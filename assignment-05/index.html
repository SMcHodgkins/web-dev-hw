Vegan French Dip

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 portobello mushroom caps
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. vegan Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp. liquid smoke
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 2-6 inch sandwich rolls or baguette sections

Directions

  1. Caramelize onions by cooking at low-medium heat in 1 tbsp of oil for 20 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 2 more minutes. Remove from pan.
  2. Slice mushrooms into thin strips. Increase pan temperature to medium and add remaining oil. Cook until lightly browned. Around 5 minutes.
  3. Return onion garlic mix to pan and add remaining ingredients. Bring to a simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove solid contents from pan, put them in your bread, and dip in sauce. Ta-da.
